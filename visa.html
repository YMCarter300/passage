Visa & Residence Permit Guide

Everything you need to know about obtaining work visas and residency permits

🇨🇦 Canada - Work Permits & PR Pathways

  • Employer-Specific Work Permit: Requires LMIA (Labour Market Impact Assessment) from employer
  • Open Work Permit: For spouses, students, or IEC participants
  • Express Entry (PR): Points-based - CRS score 470+ recommended
  • Caregiver Pilots: Home Child Care Provider, Home Support Worker - PR after 2 years
  • Processing time: 3-6 months | Cost: CAD $155 + biometrics

🇩🇪 Germany - EU Blue Card & Work Visa

  • EU Blue Card: For degree holders with job offer €45k+ (€41k for shortages)
  • Skilled Worker Visa: For vocational training or degree with job offer
  • Chancenkarte (Opportunity Card): Points-based job seeker visa - stay up to 1 year
  • Language: A1 for some visas, B1 for permanent residence
  • Processing time: 8-12 weeks | Cost: €75-100

🇦🇺 Australia - Work Visas

  • 482 Temporary Skill Shortage: Employer sponsored - 2-4 years
  • 189/190 Skilled Independent/Nominated: Points-based PR (65+ points minimum)
  • 417 Working Holiday Visa: For 18-30 - work and travel for 12 months
  • 494 Skilled Employer Sponsored: Regional work visa
  • Processing time: 3-12 months | Cost: AUD $1,500-4,000

🇬🇧 United Kingdom - Work Visas

  • Skilled Worker Visa: Job offer from approved sponsor - 70 points required
  • Health and Care Worker Visa: Reduced fees, faster processing for healthcare
  • Youth Mobility Scheme: For 18-30 from select countries - 2 years
  • Global Talent Visa: For leaders in tech, science, arts
  • Processing time: 3-8 weeks | Cost: £719-1,500 + IHS surcharge

🇦🇪 UAE - Work Visa

  • Employment Visa: Employer sponsored - 2-3 years
  • Green Visa: For skilled workers - 5 years self-sponsored
  • Freelance Visa: For remote workers and freelancers
  • Processing time: 2-4 weeks | Cost: AED 3,000-5,000

General Documents Required (Most Countries)

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Job offer letter from employer
  • Educational certificates (translated if needed)
  • Work experience letters
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical examination report
  • Proof of funds
