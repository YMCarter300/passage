Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Our Commitment

Passage is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience that respects the dignity and independence of every person who uses our platform. We believe everyone deserves equal access to global career opportunities.

Standards and Guidelines

We follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as our accessibility standard. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements. Use Tab, Shift+Tab, Enter, and Space keys to navigate.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Semantic HTML structure for proper screen reader interpretation with ARIA landmarks.
  • Color Contrast: Sufficient contrast ratios between text and background meeting WCAG AA standards.
  • Resizable Text: Text can be zoomed up to 200% without loss of functionality or content.
  • Alternative Text: Meaningful alt text for all informative images and decorative elements properly marked.
  • Focus Indicators: Visible focus outlines for keyboard users to track navigation position.
  • Clear Headings: Proper heading hierarchy for easier navigation and content comprehension.
  • Consistent Navigation: Predictable and consistent placement of navigation elements across all pages.

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to work with popular assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack, ChromeVox)
  • Screen magnification software (ZoomText, MAGic)
  • Speech recognition software (Dragon NaturallySpeaking, Windows Speech Recognition)
  • Alternative input devices (switch controls, eye tracking, on-screen keyboards)

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment that evolves with technology and user needs. We:

  • Conduct regular accessibility audits using automated tools and manual testing.
  • Train our design and development team on inclusive design and accessibility best practices.
  • Monitor user feedback to identify and address barriers quickly.
  • Include accessibility requirements in our development lifecycle from concept to deployment.
  • Perform quarterly accessibility reviews with assistive technology users.

Limitations and Alternatives

While we strive to make all content accessible, some third-party content, legacy features, or embedded widgets may not be fully accessible. If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our platform, please contact us immediately, and we will work to provide the information in an alternative format that meets your needs.

Accessibility Features Request

If you need a specific accessibility feature or alternative format (large print, braille, audio transcription, accessible PDF, or plain text), please let us know. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your needs within 5-7 business days. There is no charge for requesting accessible formats.

Third-Party Content

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services such as visa application portals, employer career pages, or government resources. While we encourage accessibility on all platforms, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of external content. We actively evaluate third-party partners for accessibility when possible.

Review and Updates

This Accessibility Statement is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect improvements and changing standards. The "Last Updated" date reflects the most recent changes. We will notify users of material changes through our website and, where appropriate, via email notifications. Historical versions are available upon request.

Evaluation Methods

Passage evaluates accessibility using a combination of automated testing (axe, Lighthouse, WAVE) and manual testing with keyboard-only navigation, screen readers (NVDA, VoiceOver), and color contrast analyzers. We also engage users with disabilities for user testing sessions bi-annually.

Formal Complaints

If you believe we have not adequately addressed an accessibility concern, you may file a formal complaint by emailing compliance@passage.com. We will acknowledge receipt within 3 business days and work toward resolution within 15 business days.
