Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience, understand how you use our site, and show you relevant content. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide valuable information to website operators.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for website functionality (login, security, navigation, load balancing) Session / Persistent
Preference Cookies Remember your settings, language preferences, and saved job filters 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (pages visited, time spent) Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Track browsing habits to show relevant job opportunities and career content Up to 180 days

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for our website to function properly. They enable core features like secure login, session management, and load balancing. Without these cookies, services you have requested (such as applying for jobs or saving searches) cannot be provided. You cannot disable these without affecting site performance and core functionality.

Analytics and Performance Cookies

We use analytics cookies to collect aggregated information about how visitors use our website. This helps us improve site structure, content, and user experience. These cookies do not identify you personally and all data is anonymized. We use this data to understand which job categories and destinations are most popular, allowing us to improve our services.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your choices, such as language preferences, job search filters, saved searches, and recently viewed positions. These cookies provide a more personalized experience and prevent you from having to re-enter your preferences each time you visit.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant job advertisements on other websites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. You can opt out of these cookies using the controls described below.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use to enhance our platform, including:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic and behavior analysis (privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy)
  • LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook: Social sharing features and job posting visibility for our employer partners
  • WhatsApp: Chat functionality to connect you with our support team
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance optimization

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and some functionality may no longer be available.

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Look for "Privacy & Security" or "Cookie Settings" in your browser menu.
  • Opt-Out Tools: Visit www.aboutads.info/choices to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent data from being used by Google Analytics.
  • Cookie Consent Tool: When you first visit our website, you can customize your cookie preferences using our cookie consent banner.

Important: Disabling essential cookies will prevent core website functionality. Disabling analytics or marketing cookies will not affect your ability to browse jobs or contact employers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. For significant changes, we may provide additional notice via email or a prominent notice on our website.
